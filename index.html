AstraX Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data Collection

AstraX does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to third parties. The application operates locally on your device and communicates only with your own configured backend server.

2. Content

AstraX is a media player application. It does not host, store, or distribute any media content. All content is sourced from M3U playlists or Xtream Codes accounts that you provide. You are solely responsible for the legality of the content you access.

3. Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device:
• Playlist URLs and names
• Device PIN (encrypted via Android Keystore)
• Favorites and hidden group preferences
• Player settings (audio/subtitle language preferences)

None of this data is transmitted to external servers other than your own backend.

4. Network Requests

AstraX makes network requests to:
• Your configured backend server (for playlist data)
• TMDB (The Movie Database) for movie/series metadata — subject to TMDB's own privacy policy
• URLs within your M3U playlists for media streaming

5. Analytics & Crash Reporting

AstraX uses Sentry for crash reporting. If the app crashes, an anonymous crash report is sent to Sentry containing the stack trace and device information (OS version, device model). No personal data, playlist URLs, or media content is included in these reports. AstraX does not include any analytics or advertising SDK.

6. Children's Privacy

AstraX includes a parental control feature that allows you to restrict content categories. The app itself does not collect data from users of any age.

7. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated with new app versions. The current version is always accessible within the app settings and at this URL.

8. Contact

For questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact us at: info@astraxplay.net
